White Water Rafting

The best trips you can find ever, just a few clicks from you!

1. The River Breeze Package

The gentle flow of tranquility.

​Ideal for families, photographers, and those seeking a peaceful escape. This trip focuses on the scenic beauty of the canyons and birdwatching while drifting through calm, crystal-clear waters.

  • Duration: 2 to 3 hours (including safety briefing).
  • ​Intensity: Class I - II (Easy / Gentle).
  • What’s Included: Professional guide, life jacket (PFD), helmet, and a waterproof bag for cameras.
  • The Experience: A relaxing journey with plenty of time for swimming in natural pools and enjoying a light snack by the riverbank.
  • Requirements: Ages 5+, no previous experience needed.

2. Wild Current Package

Feel the rhythm of the river.

​Designed for those who want the perfect mix of adrenaline and scenery. This is our most popular trip, taking you through vibrant rapids and deep forest sections where the current dictates the pace.

  • ​Duration: 4 to 5 hours (includes a riverside lunch).
  • What is Included: High-performance raft, carbon paddles, life jacket, helmet, and wetsuit (if needed).
  • The Experience: Fast-paced rapids followed by calm stretches to catch your breath.
  • You will navigate iconic drops and work as a team to master the "Wild Current."
  • Requirements: Ages 12+, must be comfortable in moving water.

​3. Mountain Pulse Package

Conquer the heart of the rapids.

Our elite expedition for the brave. This trip takes you to the highest sections of the river, where the water is coldest and the drops are steepest. It is a technical, high-energy challenge against the raw power of the mountain.

  • ​Duration: Full Day (6 to 7 hours).
  • ​Intensity: Class IV - V (Advanced / High Intensity).
  • ​What is Included: Expert expedition guide, specialized heavy-duty gear, safety kayaker escort, and a high-protein energy meal.
  • ​The Experience: Constant action. You will face narrow technical passages, large waves, and significant drops. This is about precision, strength, and the ultimate adrenaline rush.
  • ​Requirements: Ages 18+, previous Class III experience highly recommended, strong swimming skills required.
